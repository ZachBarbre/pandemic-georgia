How to Play

Pandemic Georgia is a variation of the board game Pandemic. Your goal is to gain enough knowlege to cure the pandemic in 19 days (turns).


Basics

Each player has 4 actions per turn and the player may do two actions. Movement and Cure. Four knowledge tokens are needed to win the game. The infection rate will increase as the game progresses (please note this is a future feature and not currently implemented.)

Movement

During a players turn the player may move between nearby cities by clicking on city near the currently occupied city. Due to the fact information is imperfect during an pandemic the players cannot see the exact cities that connect.

Cures

If the current player is located in a city with an infection they can click on that city to cure the infection. Cureing and infection is also how knowledge towards the cure is gained.
